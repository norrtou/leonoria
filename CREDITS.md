# Leonoria — Asset Credits

All third-party audio/art used in Leonoria and their licenses. **Every added
free asset must get a line here** (see GAMEPLAN.md phase 5).

Preferred sources: CC0 (no attribution needed, but list anyway) and CC-BY
(attribution required): Kevin MacLeod / incompetech.com, OpenGameArt.org,
Freesound.org, Kenney.nl, Pixabay Audio.

## Music
- `assets/sounds/leonoriatheme.mp3` — Leonoria theme (project-own).

## Sound effects
- `assets/sounds/*` combat SFX (project-own / origin to be documented).

## Icons
- `assets/images/weaponicons/*.svg` — weapon category icons from
  [game-icons.net](https://game-icons.net), CC BY 3.0
  (https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/3.0/). Authors: Lorc
  (sword, blade, axe, bow, chain, club-alt, fist, hammer-alt, improvised,
  mace, polearm, spear, thrown, whip, trophy) and Delapouite
  (club, flail, mounted, pick, staff). Recolored to the UI gold tone;
  black background plates removed.
