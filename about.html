What Is a Table Top Role Playing Game?

At its heart, a table top role playing game is a collaborative storytelling game powered by imagination and guided by the roll of a twenty-sided die. One player — the Game Master — acts as narrator and referee, while others portray heroes embarking on epic quests. Whether you are bartering with a fickle goblin or facing the fiery breath of a dragon, your success and your survival rest on a blend of strategy, luck, and the shared rules of the game.

The Vision

Leonoria is designed to be a complete, web-based solution for the modern adventurer. Built from the ground up using HTML, CSS, and Javascript, this platform brings the tabletop experience to your browser — no downloads, no accounts, no barriers.

A Note for the Bold

Be warned, adventurer: Leonoria is currently in early development. Like a fledgling wizard practicing their first cantrips, the experience can be a bit shaky. You may encounter bugs, and many features are still being forged in the elemental fires of our code. We are a living tapestry in progress, and your patience is the thread that helps us weave it.

Step forth, traveler. The taverns are warm, the horizon is vast, and your seat at the table is waiting.
